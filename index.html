I am a Data Engineer with over 5 years of hands-on experience in enterprise IT environments, where I worked across Windows, Linux, and Mac OS platforms supporting large-scale systems and operational workflows. My early career in IT infrastructure helped me build a strong foundation in system reliability, security, troubleshooting, and automation—skills that naturally led me toward data-focused roles.

As I transitioned into Data Engineering, I began working closely with data processing and analytics use cases. I have hands-on experience using Python and SQL to clean, transform, and validate structured datasets, build ETL workflows, and create analytics-ready data for reporting and decision-making. I have contributed to internal analytics products such as order and revenue reporting, customer activity and retention analysis, and transaction monitoring systems.

My technical skill set includes Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL (joins, CTEs, window functions), relational databases like PostgreSQL and MySQL, and tools such as Git, GitHub, Jupyter Notebook, Excel, and Power BI. I also have working knowledge of cloud and data engineering tools including AWS (S3, EC2), Apache Airflow, and Apache Spark, which I continue to strengthen through hands-on projects and the IBM Data Engineering program.

With a strong systems background and a growing focus on scalable data pipelines and automation, I am eager to contribute as a Data Engineer in a data-driven organization that values learning, ownership, and continuous improvement.
